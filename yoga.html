Yoga for Everyday Health

Beginner-friendly yoga poses to improve flexibility and calm your mind.

Mountain Pose

Stand tall with feet together, shoulders relaxed, and weight evenly on both feet.

Child’s Pose

Kneel on the floor, sit back on your heels, and stretch your arms forward on the mat.

Cat–Cow Stretch

On your hands and knees, alternate between arching and rounding your back gently.

Seated Forward Bend

Sit with legs straight and slowly bend forward, reaching towards your toes.
