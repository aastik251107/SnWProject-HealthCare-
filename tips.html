Daily Health Tips

Simple lifestyle changes can make a big difference in your health.

Stay Hydrated

Drink enough water throughout the day to keep your body and mind active.

Balanced Diet

Include fruits, vegetables, and whole grains in your meals as often as possible.

Regular Sleep

Aim for 7–8 hours of sleep to help your body recover and stay focused.

Move Your Body

Try walking, stretching, or light exercise for at least 20–30 minutes a day.
